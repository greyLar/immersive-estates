How to Deploy

        
Follow these steps once to get ImmersiveEstates live on the internet. After that, adding new properties just means adding photos in the Tour Builder.

        

          
1
Open the Tour Builder first
Add your properties and photos here in the Admin panel. Everything saves in your browser automatically — no files to manage until you're ready to go live.

          
2
Sign up for Netlify (free)
Go to netlify.com and create a free account. No credit card needed. This is where your site will live.

          
3
Deploy by drag and drop
On the Netlify dashboard, find the box that says "Deploy manually" or "drag and drop your site folder". Drag the entire immersiveestates folder into that box. Your site goes live in under 30 seconds.

          
4
Note: photos live in your browser
Right now your photos are stored in your browser (IndexedDB). For the deployed site on Netlify, photos need to be in the photos/ folder inside the project. Ask your assistant to walk you through this export step when you're ready.

          
5
Send your client their link + code
Share the tour URL with your client along with their access code from the Access Codes tab. For embed, use the Get Embed Code button inside the tour viewer.
